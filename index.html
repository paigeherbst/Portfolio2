About me

My name is Paige Herbst and I'm a 24 year old graduate student currently undertaking a Master’s program in Information and Communications Technology at the University of Denver. I'm from Minnesota, but I've had the opportunity to live in several different places across the country. My undergraduate years were spent in Queens, New York majoring in Marketing and minoring in International Studies at the St. John’s University, including studying abroad in Rome, Paris, and Dublin. After graduating and working at Amazon for almost a year, I enrolled in graduate school and began my concentration in Project Management. I'm also engaged in a women’s only coding boot camp to learn various different software languages. My overall goal is to be a Project Manager in the tech industry.
